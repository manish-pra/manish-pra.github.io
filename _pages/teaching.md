## Teaching Philosophy

I believe in **learning by doing** and **theory-practice integration**. My teaching approach emphasizes:

- 🎯 **Hands-on Learning:** Every concept is reinforced with practical implementations
- 🧠 **Critical Thinking:** Encouraging students to question assumptions and explore alternatives  
- 🤝 **Collaborative Learning:** Fostering peer-to-peer learning and group problem-solving
- 🌍 **Real-world Applications:** Connecting theoretical concepts to practical applications
