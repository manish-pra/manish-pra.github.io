Manish Prajapat

Applied Scientist at AWS | Ph.D. ETH Zurich

I build autonomous AI agents and develop theoretically grounded algorithms for sequential decision-making. My research spans agentic AI, reinforcement learning, and safe multi-agent systems, with applications to LLM-based coding agents and robotics.

I completed my Ph.D. at ETH AI Center (Dec 2025) advised by Andreas Krause and Melanie Zeilinger. Previously, I was a visiting researcher at Caltech and won Formula Student Germany & Italy 2018 with AMZ Racing.
